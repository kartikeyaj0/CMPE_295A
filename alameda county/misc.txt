Mean/Max/Min Temperature, Precipitation, Heating/Cooling days
- https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/access/monitoring/climate-at-a-glance/county/time-series/CA-001/
- Monthly data
- available since 1895

Electricity data
- total annual consumption per county available on https://ecdms.energy.ca.gov/elecbycounty.aspx
- only available since 1990

TCI data
- https://www.star.nesdis.noaa.gov/smcd/emb/vci/VH/vh_adminMean.php?type=Province_Weekly_MeanPlot
- only available for each state
- available as weekly and annually averaged data
- avaiable since 1982

Reservoir levels (CA only)
- https://cdec.water.ca.gov/reportapp/javareports?name=RES
- daily (at midnight) reservoir levels are available
- we do not know in which county a reservoir lies, and which county is served by which reservoir. Many reservoirs lie in multiple counties.
- monthly data available for each water year. Tried fetching data as back as 1973.
https://cdec.water.ca.gov/dynamicapp/QueryWY?Stations=CLE&SensorNums=15&End=2022-12-01&span=50
- Reservoir info: https://cdec.water.ca.gov/reportapp/javareports?name=ResInfo

Electricity cost per KWH-
- monthly averaged data available on US Bureau of labor statistics under CPI data.
- not available for counties
- only available for selected areas, eg: Area: San Francisco-Oakland-Hayward, CA
- available since Nov 1978
